### Dimensions given are traditional, since actual sizes often vary between copies. 
1. Folios range from 12.5" × 20" to 10" × 15". 
1. Quartos vary between 10" × 12.5" and 7.5" × 10".
1. Octavos range between 6.25" × 10" and 5" × 7.5".
